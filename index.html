What is Fit Me about?

Fit Me's goal is to reduce your time spent tracking and reflecting on your personal progress in the gym. We do this by helping you chuck out the old journal and pen. Our app allows you to create your own workouts and record how much weight you lifted and the amount of reps completed. On top of that you can upload your personal pictures to keep a timeline of your progress. All of this combined provides for a powerful experience and more accurate results with your fitness.

Check out our features

Save Time

Forget pausing after every set to write down how many reps and how much weight you just accomplished only to lose your journal a week later. You will be able to log your workout and have a digital copy as fast as you can type a text message.

Log Workouts

No more struggling to remember your workout! Add all of your exercises and then input your data as you go. Workout logging is a great way to share your program and accomplishments with others.

Add Exercises

No matter what kind of workouts you do, they can be added to the log. Freely add exercises that you are trying out if you cannot find them in the app. Afterwards, your exercise will be selectable for all future workouts!

Track Results

Keep up with your results by tracking how your numbers have improved over time along with taking a look at your timeline of progress photos. This helps you identify and correct any small problems that you may not normally be aware of.
